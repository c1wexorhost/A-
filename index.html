Enrolled Agent
The Enrolled Agent (EA) designation, provided by Prospire, is a prestigious qualification for U.S. tax professionals. EAs are IRS-authorized experts who represent individuals, businesses, and organizations in tax matters. This credential signifies top-tier expertise in tax law, making it a valuable asset for those in need of tax-related services.
