FAQ

What is CAT certification?

CAT is the Certificate in Accounting Technicians, a short-term program by ICAI.

Who can enroll in CAT?

Eligibility is 12th any stream appearing/passed students, offering comprehensive financial skills training.

How long is the CAT course?

CAT has a duration of 6 months, preparing students for entry-level accounting roles.

When are CAT exams held?

CAT exams are in June and December, featuring multiple-choice questions for assessment.

What topics does CAT cover?

CAT covers financial accounting, laws, GST, computers, costing principles, and statutory returns.

Can I pursue CAT simultaneously?

Yes, CAT can be pursued alongside higher secondary studies, enhancing employability skills.

Are there job opportunities after CAT?

CAT graduates find roles in SMEs, BPOs, tax preparation, retail, and logistics.

What is the passing standard?

To pass, secure 40% in each paper and an aggregate of 50% in the Foundation Exam.

Can I join CMA Intermediate?

Yes, eligibility for CMA Intermediate is available after completing CAT Level-1 successfully.

What are the career prospects?

CAT opens opportunities in accounting functions, SMEs, BPOs, tax preparation, and more
