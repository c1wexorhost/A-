EA

Enrolled Agent
The Enrolled Agent (EA) designation, offered by Prospire, is a prestigious qualification for U.S. tax professionals. EAs are IRS-authorized experts who represent individuals, businesses, and organizations in tax matters. This credential signifies top-tier expertise in tax law, making it a valuable asset for those in need of tax-related services.

GST VAT

Certified GST VAT Accountant
The Certified GST VAT Accountant certification from Prospire is tailored for professionals specializing in Goods and Services Tax (GST) and Value Added Tax (VAT). In many countries, GST and VAT are critical components of the tax system, and this certification equips individuals with the expertise to manage compliance, calculations, and reporting related to these taxes. Certified GST VAT Accountants are experts in these tax systems, offering valuable support to businesses in navigating complex tax regulations and ensuring compliance.

ACCA IFRS

Certified ACCA IFRS Accountant
The Certified ACCA IFRS Accountant, provided by Prospire, is for finance professionals seeking expertise in International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS). This certification ensures a deep understanding of IFRS principles and their effective application. Certified ACCA IFRS Accountants are experts in financial reporting and international accounting standards, vital for accurate financial reporting and global compliance.
